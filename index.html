About Me

I plan on contuining with the Python lessons after the bootcamp ends to try and tie in Python with web development.

Besides school and work, I enjoy rock climbing (indoors and outdoors), grew up skateboarding and although I don't skate much anymore (or was every really good), I still watch and get excited when a new skate video comes out. I live in South Pasadena, CA and love going to concerts/shows. Music has and will always be a big part of my life and who I am.
